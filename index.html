Where design meets environment

Koto is convinced that design come under environmental liability. Therefore, all of our clothes are made of flax, hemp and fluff to better respect the environment. To keep following this perspective, our clothes are made in Europe as well as our raw materials. Further than affecting environment in a positive way, our purpose is to promote local economies and industries. Japanese style, european spirit.
